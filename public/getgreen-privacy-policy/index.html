GetGreen

Privacy Policy

GetGreen is an iOS app that turns your screen time into a rough carbon-footprint estimate. Everything happens on your device.

Effective June 1, 2026 Last updated June 1, 2026
The short version. GetGreen does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no third-party SDKs, no advertising, and no servers. Nothing about your usage leaves your iPhone.

What we access

GetGreen uses Apple's Family Controls and DeviceActivity frameworks — the same private system that powers Apple's built-in Screen Time — to read aggregate screen-time totals for your device. We request your authorization on first launch via the standard system prompt.

This data is read locally by the app and a sandboxed report extension provided by iOS. We use it solely to compute the carbon estimate and equivalents (tree-days, driving distance, Arctic ice, LED bulb hours) shown on the main screen.

What we collect

Nothing. GetGreen has no backend, no analytics provider, no crash reporter, and no third-party SDKs. The app makes no network requests of any kind. Your screen-time data, your settings, and the carbon estimates derived from them never leave your device.

What we store

  • A small preferences value (whether you've chosen metric or imperial units), stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults.
  • The Family Controls authorization state, managed by iOS itself, not by us.

That's it. Both are removed when you delete the app from your device.

What we share

Nothing, with no one, for any reason. We do not sell, rent, license, trade, or otherwise disclose user data — because we do not have any.

Children's privacy

GetGreen is rated 4+ and is safe for use by anyone. Because the app does not collect any personal information from any user, it does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA / UK).

Your rights

Under privacy regulations such as the GDPR (EU/UK) and CCPA (California), you have the right to access, correct, delete, or port personal data held about you. Since GetGreen holds no such data, there is nothing for us to retrieve or delete on your behalf. You can revoke the app's Screen Time authorization at any time in Settings → Screen Time on your iPhone, and remove all local preferences by deleting the app.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date. Because GetGreen does not collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly — please check back here if you'd like to stay informed.
