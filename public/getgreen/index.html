GetGreen

Your screen time, in carbon.

An iOS app that turns your daily phone usage into a rough CO₂ estimate — and what that means in trees, miles driven, and Arctic ice. Private by design. Nothing leaves your device.

iOS 17+ 100% on-device Free

GetGreen is currently awaiting App Store approval — it'll be available to download soon.

What it does

GetGreen reads your Screen Time totals via Apple's private Family Controls framework and converts them to grams of CO₂ using a blended estimate (~20 g per hour of active screen time, covering device draw, network, and data-center work). It's a rough number — directional, not scientific — but it makes a usually-invisible cost visible.

What you see

  • ✓ Today's CO₂ — front and center, with a 30-day rolling total beneath it
  • ✓ Tree-days needed to offset your usage
  • ✓ Equivalent driving distance, in miles or kilometers
  • ✓ Arctic sea ice lost — based on Notz & Stroeve (2016), Science
  • ✓ LED bulb hours equivalent to the same emissions

Privacy

No accounts. No analytics. No third-party SDKs. No servers. The app makes no network requests of any kind. Your screen-time data stays on your phone — full stop. Read the privacy policy →
